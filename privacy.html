Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Werylet ("we", "our", or "us") operates a content platform distributing Chinese short drama series to Russian-speaking audiences. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our TikTok integration.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our content on TikTok, we may access the following information as permitted by TikTok's platform:

  • Public profile information (username, display name)
  • Content interaction data (likes, comments, shares)
  • Basic account information required for content posting

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Publish and manage video content on TikTok
  • Analyze content performance and audience engagement
  • Improve content quality and scheduling

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. We do not share data with advertisers or analytics companies beyond what is required by TikTok's platform infrastructure.

4. TikTok API Usage

Our application uses the TikTok Content Posting API exclusively for the purpose of uploading video content to our own TikTok account (@Werylet). We do not access or store data from other TikTok users.

5. Data Retention

We retain only the minimum data necessary for content management operations. Access tokens are stored securely and refreshed according to TikTok's OAuth 2.0 specifications.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any data we process. API credentials and access tokens are stored with encryption and are never shared publicly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any data we hold about you. Contact us at the email below to exercise this right.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us via Telegram: @Werylet

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
